AI Homework System is a **Homework Management + AI-Assisted Grading + Teaching Assistant Platform** designed for university courses.  
It covers the full lifecycle: **Assignment Creation → Student Submission → AI Pre-Grading → Instructor Review → Score Publishing & Analytics**.

It helps instructors focus on teaching instead of repetitive grading, and enables students to receive **fast, transparent, and traceable feedback**.

## Why Choose AI Homework System

- **AI-assisted, Instructor-controlled grading**  
  AI provides suggested scores and explanations. Instructors review, adjust, and publish final grades.

- **Fully traceable grading workflow**  
  Assignment snapshots preserve questions, answers, and grading rubrics, ensuring transparency and auditability.

- **Improved teaching efficiency**  
  Automatic submission tracking, missing submission lists, and grading progress visualization.

- **Structured Question Bank**  
  Supports textbook-chapter-question hierarchy, enabling scalable and reusable teaching resources.

- **Production-ready deployment**  
  Supports private deployment and institutional customization for real-world academic environments.

## Features Overview

### Student

- View courses and assignments
- Submit answers with **text and images**
- View grading results and historical scores
- Ask questions using AI Assistant

---

### Instructor

- Course and student management
- Assignment creation with scoring rules and deadlines
- Submission tracking and grading status monitoring
- Review AI grading suggestions and finalize scores
- Course gradebook and performance overview
- Question bank management
- Teaching assistant AI for analytics and insights

---

### Administrator

- Batch import students and courses
- Question bank governance and access control
- Institutional resource management
